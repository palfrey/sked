Getting iCal feed urls

You can use feeds from anywhere, but here's a couple of guides for popular sites.

Facebook

Goto the Facebook events page and find the "Upcoming events" link on the right hand side at the bottom. Right click and "Copy link address".

Meetup

Login to Meetup and click the "Export to" link below your calendar on the right hand side. Right click and "Copy link address" on the "iCal calendar" option

Eventbrite

Use my Eventbrite calendar exporter and copy the end URL (should start "https://eventbrite.tevp.net/calendar/").
